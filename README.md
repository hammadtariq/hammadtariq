My strength is not only writing code, but taking ownership of complex products — understanding the business goal, improving the architecture, stabilizing messy systems, and helping teams ship production-ready software.

I have worked on systems connected to companies and domains such as:

I enjoy working on products where engineering quality directly impacts business outcomes.
